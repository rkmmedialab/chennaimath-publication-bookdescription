Bhaja Govindam is one of the most popular hymns in religious literature composed by Sri Shankaracharya. The highest truths of Vedanta are highlighted lucidly in simple mnemonic verses. This book contains the text in Devanagari script along with a transliteration in Roman script and a lucid translation in English by Swami Nikhilananda. This book will aid readers understand the meaning of the Sanskrit verses.
