This is the authentic English Version of Sri Ramakrishna Lilaprasanga, a faithful record, of the life of Sri Ramakrishna Paramahamsa  by Swami Saradananda \(one of his monastic disciples\) written originally in Bengali. It combines a biography with a lucid study of the various areas of religion, mysticism and philosophy existing in India and elsewhere from the prehistoric times down to the modern era.s
