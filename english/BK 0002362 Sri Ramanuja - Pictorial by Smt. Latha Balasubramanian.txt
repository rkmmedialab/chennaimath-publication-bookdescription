Sri Ramanuja \(1017-1137 CE\) is one of the foremost of the Vedanta philosophers of India who left an indelible mark in the spiritual history of India. He rejuvenated the bhakti movement, made bhakti as a means to achieve liberation on a strong philosophical basis, and opened the doors of spirituality to one and all irrespective of caste, creed and religion.
This book, Smt. Latha Balasubramanian, presents the life of Sri Ramanuja in a simple language with colourful illustrations and will acquaint the readers with his life and mission.
